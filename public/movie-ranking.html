The Best Movies According to Harman

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Love Punjab

A heartfelt Punjabi family drama about reconnecting with your roots and rediscovering cultural identity. This one is my favourite.

Honsla Rakh

A lighthearted Punjabi romantic comedy about a single father navigating love, family, and unexpected twists.

Housefull

A Bollywood slapstick comedy full of misunderstandings, wild situations, and over-the-top humor.
